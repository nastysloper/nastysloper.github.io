Who am I?

My friends describe me as upbeat and energetic. I love learning and being able to do things I previously could not. It's a sense of accomplishment I get from climbing and from coding. When you are really pushing yourself, whether in climbing or coding, you've got to become accustomed to uncertainty; you won't improve unless you push yourself beyond your comfort zone. A "sloper" is a type of hold in climbing, and my approach to life is to make the best use of the holds I've got, no matter how tenuous.

And I love being part of both communities—smart, adventurous, quirky, friendly people.

I've created interactive web applications using Postgres, Ruby, JavaScript, Sinatra, Rails, HTML and CSS. I'm comfortable with pair programming and working in a team setting using industry standard tools like Git and RSpec.

What am I looking for?

I'm looking to work with passionate, positive people who are committed to solving real problems in the world.
